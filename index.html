FAQ

Things clients actually ask.

How long until my site is live?
Most projects launch in 1–3 weeks depending on complexity. You get a clear timeline before we start.
Will I own the website 100%?
Yes. Full ownership. Full source code. Full admin access. No vendor lock-in.
What if I want changes after launch?
First 30 days of revisions are free. After that, I offer affordable monthly support — or train you to handle it yourself.
WordPress, Shopify, or custom code — which do I need?
Depends on your goals. We discuss your project, your budget, and your team's tech comfort. Then I recommend the right platform — not the easiest one for me.
Do you use templates?
No. Every site is custom-designed and built from scratch. Templates are why your competitor's site looks like yours.
Can you handle hosting and domain?
Yes — I set it all up. Or I'll work with your current hosting. Your call.
Will my site rank on Google?
SEO foundations are built in — speed, schema, mobile, clean code. Long-term ranking depends on content + backlinks, which I can guide you on too.
What if I'm not technical at all?
Perfect. That's exactly who I work with best. I handle the tech. You focus on your business.
